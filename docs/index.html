Chris Derbyshire

Co-Founder and Director

Tim Grey

Co-Founder and Director

Tim served as a British Army officer over two periods from 1995 to 2011, initially in the Royal Corps of Signals and subsequently in the Educational and Training Services Branch of the Adjutant General’s Corps. He has excellent technical and academic credentials and a wealth of experience in learning and training development.

Joseph McMahon

Director Security Affairs
