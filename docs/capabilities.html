Our Capabilities

Tailored Solutions to Empowe Your Mission
JCAS Programme Support

We specialize in designing, planning, and implementing tailored Close Air Support (CAS) solutions. From training and equipment provision to simulation and operational support, our expertise and experience will ensure the delivery of cost-effective capability to best meet a host nations programme requirements.

EOD and C-IED

Protecting lives and ensuring safety, our Explosive Ordnance Disposal (EOD) and Counter-Improvised Explosive Device (C-IED) solutions are at the forefront of threat mitigation. Combining state-of-the-art equipment with expert training, we empower teams to effectively detect, neutralize, and dispose of explosive hazards in any environment.

Learn More
Security Risk Management.

Safeguard your operations with our comprehensive security risk management services, combining expert consultancy, personal protection, and offensive cyber security. Our tailored approach identifies and mitigates threats across physical and digital domains, ensuring robust protection for your assets and personnel in any environment.

Simulation Services

We provide and integrate mobile and fixed simulators to meet individual and collective training needs by offering platform agnostic hardware and software solutions to best fit your requirements. Incorporating extended reality (XR) where applicable, we create immersive environments for personnel to develop skills in safe, adaptable and realistic scenarios.
