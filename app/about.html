About Us

Learn More About Who We Are and What We Do

Who We Are

With a deep expertise in Joint Fires Support and Training Development, the founders of Hyperion Defence Solutions have unrivalled experience in the establishment, delivery and management of Joint Close Air Support programmes built on a foundation of successful military careers within the British Army.

What We Do

We effectively deliver military capability across all lines of development. Supported by our network of trusted advisors and subject matter experts, Hyperion Defence Solutions will conduct a needs analysis appropriate to the capability area in question before recommending a bespoke set of solutions to meet the specific needs of the host nation within the constraints and/or restrictions provided. Utilising subsidiary companies and through strategic partnerships we will deliver the solution chosen from the options presented.

Our Values

We understand the importance of transparency and honesty in establishing enduring and trusted partnerships. First and foremost, we will always endeavour to forge strong relationships with our associates based on our core values of trust, communication, integrity and reliability, to ensure everyone we work with is confident that we will only ever recommend the right solution for the right reason.
